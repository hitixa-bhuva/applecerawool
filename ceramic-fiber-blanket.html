Ceramic Fiber Blanket

High-Performance Ceramic Fiber Blanket

Reliable, lightweight, and designed for high-performance thermal protection.

The Ceramic Fiber Blanket from Apple Cera Wool is a lightweight, flexible, and highly efficient insulation solution designed for demanding high-temperature environments. Manufactured using advanced spun fiber technology, it offers exceptional thermal stability while maintaining low thermal conductivity.

Engineered to withstand temperatures of up to 1600°C, this blanket is ideal for industries looking to improve energy efficiency, reduce operating costs, and enhance workplace safety. Its unique combination of strength, flexibility, and resistance to thermal shock makes it one of the most trusted solutions for industrial insulation worldwide.
